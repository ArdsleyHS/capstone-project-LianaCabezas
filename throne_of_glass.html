In the dark, filthy salt ines of Endovier an eighteen-year-old girl is serving a life sentence. She is a trained assassin, the best of her kind, but she made a fatal mistake. She got caught.

Young Captain Westfall offers her a deal: her freedom in return for one hige sacrafice. Win or lose, she is about to discover her true destiny. But will her assassin's heart be melted?
